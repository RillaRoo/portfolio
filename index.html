About Me

Hey there!

I'm Bruno Costa, a 25-year-old game developer residing in Porto, Portugal.

My journey began in the realm of biology and care, leading me to earn a degree in nursing. While the field captivated me, my true calling drew me into the enchanting world of video game development.

Embarking on a quest to turn my childhood fascination with gaming into a career, I took the leap and dove into the realms of game design and programming at Universidade Lusófona do Porto.

Hobbies

The poetry of music

I spend a lot of my free time immersing myself in the world of video games, exploring new genres and aspects of game creation, especially music. I use tools like FL Studio and new engines to broaden my knowledge.

Writing is another passion of mine, focusing on creative themes such as philosophy, psychology and crafting narratives for video games, particularly those that delve into the human mind.

Aside from music, I'm also a member of a tuna named Tuna de Santa Maria, a musical group where many experiences and a lot of knowledge are shared. It's a place where I can grow and maintain the philosophies that have shaped me.
