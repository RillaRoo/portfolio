    "Jack's Son" is a satirical minigame inspired by the mechanics of minigames such as those from the Wario Ware series, where the player can only use one mechanic. In this case, the player is a child who must protect themselves from certain predators by throwing teddy bears at them to destroy them.


    Within a 10-second timeframe, the player must try to destroy as many enemies as possible.


    This is a game made in a comedic way but with the aim of criticizing sensitive themes about children that, unfortunately, are quite present in today's society. It was created as an experience using the Unreal 5 engine.
