    "Pace to Space" is a puzzle game created during a 48-hour game jam with the theme of "limited space". The game aims to challenge the player's mind and make them realize the patterns applied in each puzzle.


    It takes place inside a room with 2 pillars where, behind them, we have a new view of the same room. There are 4 possible views where, in each of them, the player will have a different puzzle to solve.


    After the player solves the puzzle in the room they are in, they should press the space bar button to lock in the solution. If they fail, they will have to start the game from the beginning.
